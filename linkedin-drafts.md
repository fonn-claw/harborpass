⠀
Today: **HarborPass** — marina guest check-in and amenity management.
⠀
Marinas are still running on clipboards and whiteboards.
⠀
Guests arrive, someone scribbles a slip number on a sticky note. Shower tokens get handed out and nobody tracks them. The manager has no idea what occupancy looked like last Tuesday.
⠀
HarborPass replaces all of that with a dispatch board — a single screen where staff see who's arriving, who's docked, and who's departing. Three columns. Zero guesswork.

Managers get occupancy analytics, revenue breakdowns by category, pricing configuration, and a full guest history — searchable by name or vessel.
⠀
Guests get their own portal with stay details and credentials.
